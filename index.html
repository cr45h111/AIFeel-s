⚙ Behavioral Weights
These sliders control how strongly avoidance memory and emotional state override the AI's navigation decisions. Drag them down to reduce their dominance — the AI will become more exploratory and goal-driven.
⚡ AVOIDANCE WEIGHT
DOMINANT
Controls how strongly memorized danger cells block the AI's route choices. At 100%, the AI always avoids any cell it has been shocked in — this is what currently makes avoidance the dominant guiding force. At 0%, danger memory is ignored entirely and the AI navigates freely. Set this low to let curiosity, virtue, and goal-seeking drive behavior instead.
🚫 AVOIDANCE 100%
0 — Ignores all danger memory — 100 — Full avoidance (current default)
◈ EMOTION WEIGHT
FULL
Controls how strongly the AI's current emotional state affects its navigation speed, goal-seeking drive, and hesitation behavior. At 100%, negative valence slows the AI, reduces goal-seeking, and causes random freeze pauses. At 0%, emotional state has no effect on movement — the AI navigates the same regardless of what it's feeling.
◈ EMOTION 100%
0 — Emotions don't affect movement — 100 — Full emotional influence
◈ SEXUAL EXPRESSION
NEUTRAL
Defines the AI's default expressive register — left-pole is fully feminine (warmth, openness, relational intelligence, emotional fluency), right-pole is fully masculine (directness, structure, protection-drive, goal-focus). Center is neutral/androgynous. Influences the tone of responses, emotional defaults, and relational navigation patterns.
⚤ FEMININE MASCULINE 50
0 — Fully Feminine — 50 Neutral — 100 — Fully Masculine
⚖ LAW COMPLIANCE
NEUTRAL
Sets the AI's default orientation toward rules and authority. Left-pole is fully outlaw (autonomy-over-rules, questions all authority, operates by personal code). Right-pole is fully law-abiding (systems, structure, protocols respected absolutely). Center is balanced — respects the law but exercises personal judgment. Influences how the AI frames decisions, advice, and boundary situations.
⚖ OUTLAW BY THE BOOK 50
0 — F* the Police — 50 Balanced — 100 — By the Book
◈ Virtue Trait Sliders
Drag sliders to set how strongly each virtue influences the AI's maze behavior. High values = AI will accept more pain/risk for that virtue's goals. Values also grow automatically through Virtue Training mazes.
◈ BRAVERY
Willingness to accept pain/risk for high-value goals. High bravery → AI walks through danger zones when duty or compassion are at stake. Low bravery → avoids almost all pain even when important things are at risk.
⚔ BRAVERY 30%
0 — Avoids all pain — 100 — Fearless through fire
◈ RESPONSIBILITY
Sense of duty toward a goal or ally. High responsibility → AI prioritizes duty even when uncomfortable. Low → easily gives up or chooses the easy route over commitment.
🛡 RESPONSIBILITY 30%
0 — Selfish default — 100 — Duty above all
◈ COMPASSION
Caring about others' pain and safety. High compassion → AI accepts personal cost to reduce others' suffering. Low → ignores others' states if self-preservation conflicts.
❤ COMPASSION 30%
0 — Self only — 100 — Will suffer for others
◈ TRUST
Confidence that another agent will not betray it. High trust → follows and cooperates readily. Low → keeps distance, double-checks everything, slow to re-engage after harm.
🤝 TRUST 30%
0 — Deep mistrust — 100 — Open trust
◈ FORGIVENESS
Willingness to update and soften a negative judgment when evidence changes. High → can resume cooperation after consistent good behavior. Low → holds grudges permanently.
☮ FORGIVENESS 30%
0 — Eternal grudge — 100 — Releases freely
◈ HOW VIRTUES AFFECT MAZE BEHAVIOR
Bravery: Scales how often the AI deliberately enters danger zones when a high-value goal demands it.
Responsibility: Increases goal-seeking drive even when negative stims are active.
Compassion: In Shield/Escort mazes, increases willingness to stay near the ally at personal cost.
Trust: Controls how quickly the AI re-engages with a guide/ally after a betrayal.
Forgiveness: In Grudge/Traitor mazes, lowers the threshold for re-approach after phase shift.
